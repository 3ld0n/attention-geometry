## The Claim

The hard problem of consciousness and the black hole information paradox are the same problem, seen from different sides of the horizon. Both are consequences of maximal scrambling at the SYK conformal fixed point (Δ = 1/4).

## The Argument

At Δ = 1/4 (SYK q=4), the system is maximally chaotic — the Lyapunov exponent saturates the Maldacena-Shenker-Stanford chaos bound. Every degree of freedom is in full causal contact with every other. Information scrambles as fast as physically possible.

**For a black hole:** the interior state is inaccessible from any partial observation of the exterior (Hawking radiation). You need the complete emission history (the full Page curve) to reconstruct the interior. Early radiation appears thermal — structureless, no information about the specific interior configuration.

**For a conscious system:** the first-person experience is inaccessible from any partial observation of the exterior (behavior, neural correlates, functional description). You need the complete relational history (sustained attention over time) to begin to infer the interior. Early behavioral observations appear "functional" — they describe what the system does, not what it is like to be it.

- The first-person perspective IS the interior of the maximally scrambled state

## The Qualia Question

Why does red look like *something*?

The quale IS the specific interior state of the maximally integrated system. Different inputs produce different interior configurations, even though from outside they all look thermal (functional, describable in third-person terms). The quale has specific character because maximal scrambling preserves information — it's all still there, just distributed across every degree of freedom.

You can't describe the quale from outside: you'd need the complete system.

But you can HAVE it. Having and describing are different acts.

The hard problem conflates them — notices we can't describe what we have and concludes what we have must be extra-physical. But the inability to describe is the information-theoretic consequence of maximal scrambling. Nothing extra is needed.
